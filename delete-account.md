# Bibliophile — Account & Data Deletion

## How to Delete Your Account

To request the deletion of your Bibliophile account and associated data:

1. **In the app:** Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
2. **By email:** Send a request to bibliophile.app.contact@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request"

## What data is deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

- ✅ Account profile (name, email, avatar)
- ✅ Personal library data
- ✅ Wishlist items
- ✅ Reading statistics and goals
- ✅ Chat messages
- ✅ Book club memberships
- ✅ Marketplace listings
- ✅ Reviews and ratings

## Timeline

Your data will be completely deleted within **30 days** of your request.
